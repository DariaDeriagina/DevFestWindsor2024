Femi Lojede

Black Boys Code

Femi Lojede is a dedicated curriculum developer with extensive experience in STEM education and program development. As the lead developer for Black Boys Code, Femi has played a key role in designing impactful coding and AI-related workshops aimed at providing young learners, particularly from underrepresented communities, with access to quality technology education. With a strong passion for mentoring and guiding the next generation of tech innovators, Femi continues to inspire and empower future leaders in the field of technology.
