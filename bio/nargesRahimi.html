Narges Rahimi

Narges Rahimi is a PhD candidate in the Department of Mechanical, Automotive, and Materials Engineering at the University of Windsor, Windsor, ON, Canada. Her research focuses on the security and cybersecurity of vehicles, particularly heavy-duty vehicles, by exploring industry standards and frameworks. She has a background in electrical engineering and specializes in system engineering, risk assessment, and cybersecurity within the automotive sector. Narges is also a Research Assistant at the SHIELD Automotive Cybersecurity Centre of Excellence, where she contributes to advancing security strategies for modern automotive systems, with a focus on the unique cybersecurity challenges of heavy-duty vehicles.
