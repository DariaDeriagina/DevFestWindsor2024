Ahmed Zaky

Genio-Tech Robotics

Ahmed Zaky founded Genio-Tech Robotics, a leading robotics academy in Alexandria, Egypt, in 2014. With a passion for empowering young minds through technology, Ahmed has expanded Genio-Tech’s reach globally, opening branches in Dubai in 2020 and, most recently, in Windsor, Ontario. Genio-Tech Robotics has since become a trusted name in robotics and coding education, renowned for its innovative, hands-on curriculum tailored for kids and teens. Under Ahmed’s leadership, Genio-Tech continues to shape the future of STEM education, offering unique learning experiences that inspire creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills in the next generation.
